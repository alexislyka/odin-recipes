Tomato and Eggs

A juicy weeknight side dish that you can whip up in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb tomato
  • 4 egg
  • 5 slice ginger
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch (for slurry)
  • 4 tbsp water (for slurry)
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 0.50 tsp salt (for eggs)
  • 0.50 tsp salt (for stir-fry)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar (for stir-fry)
  • 2 tbsp ketchup (for stir-fry)

Steps

  1. Cut each tomato in half and remove the hard bit at the stem. Cut each half further into 4 wedges.
  2. Cut 5 thin slices of ginger (5 slice), and then julienne into thin strips.
  3. Crack the eggs (4 ) into a large bowl. Season it with salt (0.50 tsp), and then beat vigorously until well combined.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together cornstarch (1 tbsp) and water (4 tbsp).

  5. Add oil and give the pan a swirl to coat. Pour in the beaten eggs, and push the eggs gently around to scramble.

    When the eggs are about 80% cooked through, transfer them to a temporary plate or bowl.

  6. Add the julienned ginger, then the tomato wedges. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds, then add water. Season with salt (0.50 tsp) and brown sugar (2 tbsp). Keep the tomatoes cooking for about 2.5 minutes.
  7. Add the eggs back into the pan. Stir it in and let cook for 50 seconds, then turn off the heat. Add the ketchup (2 tbsp) and mix it in.
  8. Scoop or pour it out into a serving dish, and enjoy!
