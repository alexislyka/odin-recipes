Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish marinated in soy sauce and other spices, pan-fried, and boild until tender.

Ingredients

  • 1/2kg chicken
  • 4tbsp soy sauce
  • 4tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 garlic
  • 2tbsp brown sugar
  • Knorr chicken cube
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Start by adding a bit of oil and stir-fry 1/3 of the garlic and chicken.
  2. Once the chicken is half cooked, add the soy sauce, vinegar, water, and bay leaves, then let boil.
  3. Add the sugar, chicken cube, and black pepper.
  4. Continue boiling to the desired sauce consistency, and serve!
